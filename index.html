About Us

Founded in 2024 by two passionate teenagers, the Jhapa Arm Wrestling Federation (JAWF) is the pioneer of organized arm wrestling in Jhapa, Nepal. We aim to bring out raw talent, build champions, and uplift the arm wrestling scene both locally and nationally.

Our mission is to empower athletes through community, training, and fair competition.

Weekly Training

Join us every Sunday in Damak for open practice pulls and training sessions. Whether you’re a beginner or a pro, these sessions are perfect to improve your skills and strength with guidance from local athletes.
